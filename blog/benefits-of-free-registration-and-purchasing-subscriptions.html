Benefits of free registration

If you choose to register with your e-mail address, you can activate multiple devices with the same account by taking each device and logging into the same account. We currently support all Apple devices such as iPhones, iPads and Macs.

Having an account gives you multiple benefits, such as access to a larger pool of free servers that are less busy. Additionally, you will be utilising our V5 engine, which is far superior in code quality than our previous V4 engine. Last but not least, we can provide you with a much better customer service experience. The benefits are even greater when you subscribe.

Benefits of purchasing subscriptions

We offer two types of subscriptions: Lite & Standard.  Purchasing any form of subscription leads to an advertisement free experience, but it also offers further benefits.

If you purchase ‘Lite’, you can activate the subscription on unlimited number of iPhones and iPad devices. ‘Lite’, however, only allows one device to be active at a time. Within 300 seconds, any other devices become disconnected from the system.

If you purchase our ‘Standard’ subscription, you can similarly activate it on an unlimited number of iPhones, iPads and Mac devices. ‘Standard’, however, allows up to three devices to remain active. Any device that was logged in before the previous active three, becomes automatically disconnected from the server.

Finally, both premium subscriptions allow you to select your preferred server anywhere in the world.
