Introducing A Stealth VPN Technology

On 7th July 2020, Emirates Telecommunication blocked our VPN on their home broadband packages. We investigated, over many weeks, how we could mitigate the situation. We reached out to our users and ran a number of tests with local volunteers.

Tegant Stealth VPN

We then began development on an alternative VPN technology, which we will not name here publicly. After seven weeks of development we discovered a way of hiding new VPN traffic and circumventing the blockage. We cannot guarantee that they will not find a way to block us again, but with the experience gained, we now know how to change our footprint.

The VPN game is a game of cat and mouse. There are governments out there that, for self-interested reasons, do not want their citizens to have access to a free, unfettered internet. And then there is us, who strive to oppose such restriction and suppression, and promote freedom of both expression and information.
